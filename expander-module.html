CeLAN Expansion Module

CeEX-DIN

 

The CINCH Systems CeLAN Expander Module (EX) mounted on a DIN rail, simplifies your installation by allowing you to interconnect up to 10 different CeLAN devices / wire-runs (+12 VDC, Bus A, Bus B, GND), on separate terminals. It also provides three sets of additional power and ground terminals for other powered devices. The module can be mounted into the Protector and Protector II Enclosure, Protector and Protector II Accessory Enclosure, or Custom Plastic CeLAN Enclosure w/wall & cover tamper.

Features:

  • Ten 4-position terminal blocks for CeLAN Expansion (+12 VDC, Bus A, Bus B, GND).
  • Three 2-position terminal blocks (+12 VDC, GND), for power expansion to other powered devices.
  • Mounts inside Protector and Protector II, Protector and Protector II Accessory, or Enclosure- CeLAN Custom Plastic w/wall & cover tamper.
  • Includes Accessory hardware kit.
