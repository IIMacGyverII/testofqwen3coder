Compatriot Vehicle Barrier Control System

CCU-VBS

 

Designed for high security and vehicle barrier operations.  With similar features of the larger capacity Rampart—the Compatriot is scaled-down, yet still designed to meet the high security needs of vehicle barrier, door, and gate operations at smaller facilities.

 

The Compatriot VBS delivers advanced AES encrypted system protection for both new and existing high security vehicle barrier installations. Compatriot's encrypted expansion capability provides patented, 100% secure 128-bit AES encrypted communication between all encrypted devices connected to the system.

 

Utilizing the Compatriot control panel and VBS support modules, the system is capable of controlling and monitoring up to 10 vehicle barriers, bollards, gate arms, traffic lights, rolling gates, and much more!

Product Applications:

Government Agencies, Sensitive Compartmented Information Facilities - SCIF’s, Command Centers. Critical Infrastructure, Embassies, High Security Critical Industrial, Energy Infrastructure, Transportation, Financial Institutions and any facility requiring the highest level of encrypted security.
